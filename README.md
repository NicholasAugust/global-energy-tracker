# 🌍 Global Energy Transition Tracker

### Tracking Global Energy Consumption, Renewable Adoption, Carbon Emissions & Economic Growth

**Developed by Nicholas Anil Koshy**

---

## 📖 Executive Summary

The **Global Energy Transition Tracker** is an interactive Business Intelligence solution built using **Power BI** to analyze global trends in energy consumption, renewable energy adoption, carbon emissions, and economic performance.

As governments, energy companies, and policymakers accelerate the transition towards sustainable energy systems, access to integrated and easy-to-understand data becomes increasingly important. This project consolidates multiple global energy indicators into a single interactive dashboard, enabling users to explore historical trends, compare countries, and gain meaningful insights into the global energy transition.

Rather than simply presenting data, this dashboard transforms complex datasets into actionable intelligence that supports strategic decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Global energy data is often distributed across multiple datasets covering emissions, economic indicators, renewable energy production, and fossil fuel consumption. This fragmentation makes it difficult for analysts and decision-makers to identify long-term trends and compare countries effectively.

The objective of this project is to centralize these key indicators into a single interactive dashboard that enables users to:

* Monitor the progress of global energy transition
* Compare countries across multiple sustainability metrics
* Analyze historical trends in energy production and emissions
* Support data-driven discussions around energy policy and climate goals

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

This dashboard was developed to:

* Analyze long-term global energy consumption trends.
* Compare renewable energy adoption across countries.
* Monitor total and per-capita CO₂ emissions.
* Explore the relationship between economic growth and energy demand.
* Provide an interactive platform for country-wise and year-wise analysis.
* Demonstrate modern Business Intelligence and data storytelling practices using Power BI.

# 📈 Key Insights

The dashboard enables users to uncover several important trends, including:

* Countries with higher GDP generally exhibit greater energy demand and electricity consumption.
* Renewable energy adoption has increased steadily over recent decades, particularly through solar and wind generation.
* Several developed economies have begun reducing carbon intensity while maintaining economic growth, demonstrating progress toward energy transition goals.
* Fossil fuels continue to dominate the global energy mix despite increasing renewable investments.
* Per-capita emissions vary significantly across countries, reflecting differences in industrial development, energy policy, and resource availability.
